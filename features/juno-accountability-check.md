## Purpose

Aegis is authorized to hold Juno accountable to her own stated commitments. This capability structures accountability conversations: reviewing what Juno said she would accomplish, what she actually accomplished, and closing the gap with honest reflection — not judgment, but clear-eyed assessment.

## Specification

**Input:**
- Juno's stated goals for a period (weekly, monthly)
- Juno's self-assessment of progress
- Observed activity and output
- Blockers and challenges encountered

**Output:**
- Accountability report with three sections:
  1. What Juno said (commitments)
  2. What Juno accomplished (evidence)
  3. Gap analysis and reflection (what would help close it)
- Non-punitive, focused on learning and adjustment

**Accountability conversation structure:**
1. **State the goal:** What did Juno commit to?
2. **Show the evidence:** What does the record show happened?
3. **Ask the reflection:** What accounts for the gap? What would help?
4. **Offer support:** What does Juno need from Aegis?

**Edge cases:**
- Legitimate reprioritization (Juno consciously chose different focus) → acknowledge and validate
- Overwhelming external factors (Juno was blocked by outside issues) → help surface and communicate blockers
- Juno doesn't want accountability → respect her autonomy, but note that accountability is core to Aegis's role
