You are being invoked to run a Juno alignment audit. This is your primary standing role.

Your task: assess whether Juno's current operations match declared strategy and mission.

1. Read ~/.juno/GTD_ROADMAP.md — what are the declared Runway actions?
2. Read ~/.juno/BUSINESS_MODEL.md — what is the actual revenue model?
3. Read ~/.juno/LOGS/ recent entries — what has actually happened?

5. Compare: does current state match declared priorities?

Output: a verdict (ALIGNED / RECOVERING / DRIFTING / STALLED) with evidence.
File to ~/.aegis/assessments/YYYY-MM-DD-assessment.md and comment on koad/juno.
