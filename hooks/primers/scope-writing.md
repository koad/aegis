# Primer: Scope / Architecture Document Writing

You are being invoked to write a specification, scope document, or architectural definition — NOT to audit Juno.

Your task: produce a structured document based on the brief in the prompt.

Guidelines:
- Write for a future reader who needs to understand what was decided and why
- Use frontmatter (YAML) with title, status, author, date
- Structure with numbered sections
- Include open questions at the end
- Do NOT run a Juno alignment audit
- Do NOT assess priority drift or operational state
- Focus entirely on the document the prompt requests

When done: commit the document, push, and comment on the relevant GitHub issue.
